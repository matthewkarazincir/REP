Curriculum

As the fields of science, more specifically engineering, continue to grow at a rapid pace, STEM marks itself as the core field that drives our world forward. The enrichment program acts as a tool of inspiration for students to be leaders and innovators of the future generation.

The curriculum is as listed below:

  • Day #1: Basic Maneuvers - Introduction to gears and sensors.
  • Day #2: Lifting + Carrying objects, Precise angles.
  • Day #3: Using Sensors to follow colored lines + navigate a maze
  • Day #4: LEGO FIRST Practice Challenge - Students will build a robot to accomplish a variety of tasks on a LEGO FIRST demo map/field.
  • Day #5: Sumo Bots - Tournament for students to experiment with more freedom, utilizng techniques which have been taught
