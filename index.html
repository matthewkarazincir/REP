Inspiring Leaders of Science and Technology

In the Enrichment Program, we strive towards inspiring the youth in STEM for a better tomorrow with the help of early learning robotics technology.

Every summer, participating members of the FIRST Robotics team AwtyBots 5829 will hold camps at partnering schools. Get involved today and prepare your child for a better tomorrow.
